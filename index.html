Most of what eats your engineering budget is not model work. It is orchestration.

Here is the lens we would bring to your stack. Teams who skip this triage put language models where Postgres would do the job faster and cheaper, and they run out of runway before the ten percent work ships.

60%

Traditional code and database work

Export presets, title slides, scheduler plumbing, billing edge cases. Ships faster as ordinary software.

30%

Rule-based logic

"If clip length under fifteen seconds and speaker count equals one, skip the reframe pass." No LLM required.

10%

Genuine AI

Highlight scoring model, multilingual transcription quality, studio voice. Where the team's scarce cycles actually belong.

We would sit with the Wisecut team for a day, map each open roadmap item onto the right layer, and give you a written orchestration design that your next hire can read on day one.
